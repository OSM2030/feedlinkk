Feeding India

Feeding India, powered by Zomato, is dedicated to creating a hunger-free India through daily meal services for the underserved. This initiative strives to tackle food insecurity by redistributing surplus food to communities in need, thus helping minimize food wastage. In partnership with Zomato, Feeding India harnesses a vast network of volunteers, donors, and technology to provide meals, essential nutrition, and hope to countless individuals each day. Through this initiative, Zomato aims to bridge the gap between surplus food and those struggling with hunger, turning food that would otherwise go to waste into vital nourishment for the community.

Benefits Of Food Donation

Food donation goes beyond charity—it’s a sustainable solution to reduce waste and uplift communities. Discover the holistic impact of food donation and how you can be a part of this transformative movement towards a sustainable and inclusive society.

Stop Food Waste

Reducing food waste is a crucial step toward environmental conservation and hunger reduction.
